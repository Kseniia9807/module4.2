potok field recordings (PFR) is a project focusing on recording ambient music. We think that
instruments that we use for recordings should suit our ambitions and goals, so we decided to create
our own collection of music gear and our own space to make our working process more
convenient. Now we decided to share this project with you.

Glacier
portable, polyphonic synthesizer designed for ambient
textures and soundscapes. Features include a responsive
keyboard, a collection of evolving pads, shimmering
ambient textures, and granular synthesis capabilities.
